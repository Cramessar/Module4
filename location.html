Directions

By Train:

  • Take the A, C, or E subway lines to 34th Street - Penn Station and walk west to the Javits Center.
  • The 7 subway line stops at 34th Street - Hudson Yards, a short walk from the venue.

By Bus:

  • M34-SBS and M12 buses stop near the Javits Center.
  • Check the MTA website for schedules and real-time updates.

By Ride Sharing:

  • Uber and Lyft are available throughout New York City. Use the drop-off location at 11th Avenue and 36th Street for easy access to the venue.
  • Local taxi services are also available and can be hailed on the street or booked via app.
